Questions

Common Questions

Is this for beginners?
Yes. The programme is designed entirely around your current level. Whether you've never set foot in a gym or you've been training for years, everything starts from where you actually are — not where you think you should be.
What if I've never trained properly before?
If you've been trying to figure it out on your own and not getting anywhere, that's not a failure — it's a missing piece. That's what a coach is for. We start at the right level and build from there.
How is this different from having a PT at my gym?
At a commercial gym you share the space with everyone else. Your PT might be distracted, competing for equipment, or running between clients. Here, the gym is yours for the entire session. One client, one coach, no interruptions. It fundamentally changes the quality of the work.
Can I try before committing?
Yes. The first step is a free consultation — a 20–30 minute conversation to understand your goals, your history, and whether this is the right fit. No obligation, no hard sell. If it makes sense for both of us, we move forward. If not, no problem.
Where is the gym based?
Newton Mearns, Glasgow — accessible from Giffnock, Clarkston, Whitecraigs, Barrhead, East Renfrewshire, and South Glasgow. Exact location is shared once you've booked your consultation.
Does nutrition coaching cost extra?
No. Nutritional guidance is included as part of the service. You can't out-train a bad diet, so it doesn't make sense to coach someone's training and ignore everything else. You get the full picture included.
